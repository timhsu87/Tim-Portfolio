FLOWER STUDIO: Floral Shop Website
HTML  CSS   Adobe XD
The Plan
This flower shop landing page is created for a friend who owns a flower shop. I decided to take on this project to help her showcase some of her products. What I have created is a template for now which will be added with real products and content later on. The landing page will incorporate various content such as gallery and blog posts.
The Process
I started with a wireframe in Adobe XD and created the sections separately. This was a more challenging site to create as there are many design styles that are more complicated but it is a good test for my coding knowledge. Through the process I had a stronger understanding of CSS and was able to create and use this as a foundation for future projects.
Adobe XD Wireframe
Design
The design was created to appeal to the more feminine customers as most of the flowers are normally given to females. Pink and other lighter tones were chosen to accentuate the flower and natural feeling of the site. Use of space and layout was chosen to give the visitors a clear understanding of which section they are in. Both the typography, colors, and design were chosen to create a relaxed and boutique feeling.
